<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera module market is primarily divided into two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS sensors are known for their lower power consumption, faster readout speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for smartphones and consumer electronics. In contrast, CCD sensors excel in image quality and low-light performance, traditionally utilized in professional cameras and scientific applications. Both technologies cater to different segments, driving innovation and competition within the camera module market.</p></p>
