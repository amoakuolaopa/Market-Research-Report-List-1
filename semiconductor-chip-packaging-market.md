<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chip Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging(FO WLP)</li><li>Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging(FI WLP)</li><li>Flip Chip(FC)</li><li>2.5D/3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor chip packaging market features several advanced technologies. Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP) allows for a higher density of connections and a smaller footprint, enhancing performance. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP) offers a more traditional approach, with die placed on a larger substrate. Flip Chip (FC) technology connects the chip directly to the substrate, allowing for better electrical performance and thermal management. Finally, 2.5D/3D packaging integrates multiple chips in a single package, improving performance and space efficiency by stacking dies.</p></p>
