Other metal-organic (MO) sources include compounds like trimethylantimony or dimethylzinc, catering to niche applications. These compounds are critical in chemical vapor deposition (CVD) and metal-organic chemical vapor deposition (MOCVD) processes for advanced material synthesis.</p></p>
