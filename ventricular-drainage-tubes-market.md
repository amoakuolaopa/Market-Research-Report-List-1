<p><p>The ventricular drainage tubes market is categorized primarily into two types: ventricular and peritoneal drainage systems. Ventricular drainage tubes are designed to alleviate intracranial pressure by draining excess cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) from the ventricles of the brain. In contrast, peritoneal drainage systems are used to divert fluid accumulation within the abdominal cavity. Both types play critical roles in managing conditions like hydrocephalus and other fluid-related disorders, significantly impacting patient outcomes and improving quality of life.</p></p>
