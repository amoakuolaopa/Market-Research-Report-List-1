<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Articulated Arm Machines</li><li>Electric Articulated Arm Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The articulated arm machine market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic and electric articulated arm machines. Pneumatic articulated arm machines utilize compressed air for operation, offering lightweight and flexible movement ideal for various industrial applications. On the other hand, electric articulated arm machines employ electric motors, providing greater precision and control for complex tasks. Both types cater to different operational needs, with pneumatic machines often favored for speed and portability, while electric machines excel in accuracy and strength.</p></p>
