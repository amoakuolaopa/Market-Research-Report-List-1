Sperm banking, on the other hand, involves collecting and freezing sperm from donors or individuals, ensuring availability for assisted reproductive technologies. Both services cater to individuals and couples facing fertility challenges or those seeking to plan parenthood strategically, enhancing reproductive options.</p></p>
