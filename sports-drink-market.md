<p><strong>The Sports Drink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports drink market is categorized into three main types: hypotonic, isotonic, and hypertonic drinks. Hypotonic sports drinks contain a lower concentration of electrolytes and carbohydrates, making them ideal for quick hydration after light activities. Isotonic sports drinks match body fluid concentration, delivering hydration and replenishing electrolytes and energy during moderate workouts. Hypertonic sports drinks have a higher concentration of sugars and electrolytes, suitable for recovery and energy replenishment after intense physical exertion. Each type serves distinct hydration and nutritional needs.</p></p>
