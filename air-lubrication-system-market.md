<p><p>The Air Lubrication System market encompasses two main types: Air Films and Microbubbles. Air Films involve the generation of a continuous layer of air beneath a vessel, reducing friction and enhancing fuel efficiency. Microbubbles, on the other hand, utilize smaller air bubbles dispersed in water to create a lubricating layer that minimizes resistance during movement. Both types aim to improve vessel performance, decrease fuel consumption, and lower emissions, making them important innovations in maritime technology.</p></p>
