<p><ul><li>Pile Foundation</li><li>Leg Post</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind jackets market is divided into two primary types: pile foundation and leg post. Pile foundation wind jackets utilize deep, strong piles driven into the seabed, providing stability for offshore structures in harsh marine environments. Leg post wind jackets, on the other hand, consist of sturdy legs anchored to the seafloor, offering a robust framework for supporting wind turbines. Both types aim to enhance energy generation efficiency and resilience against environmental challenges, catering to the growing demand for renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
