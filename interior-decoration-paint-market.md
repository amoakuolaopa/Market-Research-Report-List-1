<p><strong>The Interior Decoration Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solvent-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior decoration paint market is primarily categorized into two types: waterborne and solvent-borne paints. Waterborne paints utilize water as a solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound emissions, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. They offer quick drying times and excellent adhesion. In contrast, solvent-borne paints contain organic solvents, providing superior durability and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and application needs.</p></p>
