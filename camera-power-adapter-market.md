<p><p>The camera power adapter market is primarily segmented into two types: AC adapters and DC adapters. AC adapters convert alternating current from wall outlets into a stable power source for cameras, making them ideal for studio settings or prolonged use. In contrast, DC adapters provide direct current, often utilized for on-the-go power supply from batteries or automotive sources, catering to photographers who need portability. Both types meet different power requirements and usage scenarios, enhancing versatility for camera operations.</p></p>
