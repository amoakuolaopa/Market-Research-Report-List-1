<p><strong>The Power Transmission Lines and Towers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Tension</li><li>Extra High Tension</li><li>Ultra High Tension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power transmission lines and towers are categorized based on voltage levels, which determine their capacity to transmit electricity over long distances. High Tension (HT) lines operate at voltages between 33 kV and 132 kV, suitable for regional distribution. Extra High Tension (EHT) lines range from 132 kV to 765 kV, supporting extensive transmission networks. Ultra High Tension (UHT) lines exceed 765 kV, enabling efficient long-distance transport of large power volumes, minimizing energy losses and enhancing grid reliability.</p></p>
