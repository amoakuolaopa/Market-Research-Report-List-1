<p><p>Anaerobic thread sealants are designed to prevent leakage in threaded metal connections. The market can be categorized into three types based on strength: </p><p>1. **Low Strength** sealants are suitable for applications requiring easy disassembly, such as plumbing and maintenance tasks. </p><p>2. **Medium Strength** sealants provide a balanced solution for general purpose use, offering adequate resistance to vibrations while still allowing for disassembly. </p><p>3. **High Strength** sealants are ideal for permanent joints, with exceptional bonding capabilities to withstand extreme conditions and heavy loads.</p></p>
