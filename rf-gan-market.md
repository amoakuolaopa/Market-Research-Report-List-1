<p><strong>The RF GaN Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gan-On-Sic</li><li>Gan-On-Silicon</li><li>Gan-On-Diamond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF GaN market encompasses three main types of GaN substrates: GaN-on-SiC, GaN-on-Silicon, and GaN-on-Diamond. GaN-on-SiC utilizes silicon carbide for its excellent thermal conductivity and power efficiency, making it ideal for high-frequency applications. GaN-on-Silicon offers cost advantages and is suitable for consumer electronics, though it has lower thermal performance. GaN-on-Diamond, emerging as a high-performance solution, provides superior thermal management and is targeted at advanced applications such as 5G and satellite communications.</p></p>
