<p><p>The Steel Flange Forming Machine market is primarily segmented into Simplex and Duplex types. Simplex machines operate on a single forming process, typically suitable for low-volume production, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Duplex machines utilize a dual-forming process, allowing for higher efficiency and production volume. Duplex systems are ideal for larger-scale operations, as they can produce more complex flanges simultaneously, thereby reducing cycle times and increasing overall throughput in industries requiring substantial flange fabrication.</p></p>
