<p><p>Ceramic milling cutters are used in various industries for precision cutting and machining. The market is categorized into three main types: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based milling cutters. Iron-based cutters are favored for their strength and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for general applications. Nickel-based cutters offer high-temperature resistance, ideal for aerospace and automotive sectors. Cobalt-based cutters provide exceptional wear resistance and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on material properties.</p></p>
