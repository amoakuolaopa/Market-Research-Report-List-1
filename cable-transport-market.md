Surface transport includes cable-driven systems like funiculars that move along the ground, often utilized in steep terrains. Vertical transport encompasses elevators and escalators, utilizing cables to move passengers or goods between different levels, ensuring accessibility in buildings and transportation hubs.</p></p>
