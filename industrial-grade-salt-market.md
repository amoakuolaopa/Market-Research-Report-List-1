<p><p>The industrial-grade salt market comprises three main types: sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is harvested from evaporated seawater, known for its purity and mineral content. Well and rock salt are sourced from underground deposits; well salt is mined from saline aquifers while rock salt is directly extracted from salt mines. Lake salt is derived from the evaporation of saline lakes, often containing unique mineral profiles. Each type serves various industrial applications, including chemicals, food processing, and de-icing.</p></p>
