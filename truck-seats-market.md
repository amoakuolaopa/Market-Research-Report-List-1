<p><strong>The Truck Seats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Suspension Truck Seats</li><li>Mechanical Suspension Truck Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck seats market is primarily divided into two types: air suspension and mechanical suspension truck seats. Air suspension seats utilize an air bladder system to provide a smooth ride by absorbing shocks and vibrations, enhancing comfort over long distances. In contrast, mechanical suspension seats rely on metal springs and dampers for support, offering a more traditional, yet effective, method of shock absorption. Both types aim to improve driver comfort and reduce fatigue, catering to diverse operational needs in the trucking industry.</p></p>
