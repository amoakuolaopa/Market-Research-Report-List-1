<p><ul><li>Silicon</li><li>Organic and Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2D interposer market comprises three main types: silicon, organic, and glass. Silicon interposers offer excellent electrical performance and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-density applications. Organic interposers are lighter and more flexible, catering to consumer electronics and cost-sensitive markets. Glass interposers provide superior dielectric properties and lower signal loss, suitable for high-frequency applications. Each type serves distinct needs in advanced packaging technologies, enabling efficient integration of multiple components in systems-on-chip (SoCs) and heterogeneous assemblies.</p></p>
