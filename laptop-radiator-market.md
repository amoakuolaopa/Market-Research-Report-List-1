<p><strong>The Laptop Radiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Laptop Radiator</li><li>Passive Laptop Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laptop radiator market is categorized into two main types: active and passive radiators. Active laptop radiators utilize fans or pumps to enhance airflow and cooling efficiency, often featuring advanced technologies such as liquid cooling systems. In contrast, passive laptop radiators rely on natural convection and heat dissipation without any moving parts, making them quieter and often more energy-efficient. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal temperatures and performance in laptops, catering to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
