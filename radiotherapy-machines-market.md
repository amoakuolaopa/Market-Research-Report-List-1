<p><strong>The Radiotherapy Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy</li><li>Systemic Radiotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiotherapy machines market comprises three main types: External Beam Radiotherapy (EBRT), Internal Beam Radiotherapy (also known as brachytherapy), and Systemic Radiotherapy. EBRT delivers targeted radiation from outside the body to treat tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Internal Beam Radiotherapy involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor, maximizing localized treatment. Systemic Radiotherapy administers radioactive substances through the bloodstream to target cancer cells throughout the body, offering a comprehensive approach for widespread disease.</p></p>
