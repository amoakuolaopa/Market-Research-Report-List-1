<p><ul><li>Molded-case Circuit Breaker (MCCB)</li><li>Air Circuit Breaker (ACB)</li><li>Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low voltage circuit breaker market encompasses three main types: Molded-case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), Air Circuit Breaker (ACB), and Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB). MCCBs provide protection against overloads and short circuits in medium voltage applications. ACBs are designed for high current ratings and can interrupt short circuits with a quick response. MCBs, on the other hand, are used for low voltage applications, offering reliable protection against overcurrent with a compact design for residential and commercial use.</p></p>
