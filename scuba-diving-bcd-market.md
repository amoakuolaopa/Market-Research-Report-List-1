<p><p>The scuba diving BCD (Buoyancy Control Device) market features three main types: Jacket Type, Back-Inflate, and Wing Type. Jacket Type BCDs encase the diver, offering stability and comfort, ideal for beginners. Back-Inflate BCDs provide streamlined profiles, enhancing mobility and reducing drag, favored by experienced divers. Wing Type BCDs consist of a wing-like bladder attached to a harness, promoting efficient buoyancy control, often used in technical diving. Each type caters to different diving preferences and experience levels.</p></p>
