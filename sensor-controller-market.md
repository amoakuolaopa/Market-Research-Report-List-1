<p><p>The sensor controller market is categorized into two main types: single-sensor controllers and multi-sensor controllers. Single-sensor controllers are designed to manage and process data from a single type of sensor, providing a focused and efficient solution for specific applications. In contrast, multi-sensor controllers integrate data from multiple sensor types, allowing for more comprehensive monitoring and control capabilities. This versatility enables them to handle complex environments and applications, making them ideal for smart buildings, industrial automation, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
