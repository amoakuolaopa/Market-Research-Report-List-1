<p><p>Chlorine scrubbers are essential systems used to remove chlorine gas from emissions in various industrial processes. The market is primarily divided into two types: dry scrubbers and wet scrubbers. Dry scrubbers utilize solid sorbents to absorb and neutralize chlorine, offering a cost-effective and efficient solution with minimal wastewater. Wet scrubbers, on the other hand, use liquid solutions to capture and dissolve chlorine, providing effective scrubbing but potentially generating wastewater. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational needs and environmental regulations.</p></p>
