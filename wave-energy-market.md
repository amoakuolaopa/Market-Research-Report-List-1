<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wave Energy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oscillating Water Column</li><li>Oscillating Body Converters</li><li>Overtopping Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wave energy market comprises three primary converter types. Oscillating Water Columns (OWC) utilize trapped air rising and falling with waves to drive turbines. Oscillating Body Converters harness the kinetic energy of floating or submerged devices that move with wave motion. Overtopping Converters capture incoming waves in a reservoir, allowing water to flow back to the sea through turbines. Each type offers distinct operational mechanisms and efficiencies, contributing to the diversification of renewable energy solutions from ocean waves.</p></p>
