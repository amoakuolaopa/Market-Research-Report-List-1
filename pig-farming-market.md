<p><ul><li>Farrow-to-finish farms</li><li>Farrow-to-nursery farms</li><li>Farrow-to-wean farms</li><li>Wean-to-finish farms</li><li>Finishing farms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pig farming market types include various operations based on the production cycle. Farrow-to-finish farms manage pigs from breeding to market weight, ensuring comprehensive control over the process. Farrow-to-nursery farms focus on breeding and raising piglets until weaning, while farrow-to-wean farms specialize in breeding and selling weaned piglets. Wean-to-finish farms take weaned pigs and raise them to market weight. Finally, finishing farms concentrate solely on growing pigs from weaning to finishing, optimized for meat production efficiency.</p></p>
