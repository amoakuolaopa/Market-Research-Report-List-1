<p><strong>The Quantitative Dispensing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric</li><li>Weight Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantitative dispensing machine market comprises two primary types: volumetric and weight-based machines. Volumetric machines measure and dispense liquids based on a set volume, ensuring accuracy in fluid dispensing for applications like beverages or chemicals. In contrast, weight-based machines dispense products by measuring weight, making them suitable for solid and granular materials, such as powders or grains. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing efficiency and precision in the dispensing process while minimizing waste and manual handling.</p></p>
