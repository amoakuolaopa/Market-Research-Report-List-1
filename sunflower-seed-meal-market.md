<p><p>The sunflower seed meal market encompasses three primary types: pellets, powder, and cakes. Pellets are compact, easy-to-handle forms that facilitate storage and transport, often used in animal feeds. Powder is a finely ground form, offering versatility for various applications, including dietary supplements and food ingredients. Cakes are the solid residue after oil extraction, rich in protein and fiber, primarily utilized in livestock feeding. Each type serves distinct needs in agriculture and food industries, highlighting the meal's adaptability and nutritional value.</p></p>
