<p><p>The resin optical lens market is categorized into three refractive index types: low, medium, and high. Low refractive index lenses typically have a refractive index below 1.53, offering basic performance and affordability. Medium refractive index lenses range from 1.53 to 1.60, providing a balance between thickness, weight, and optical quality. High refractive index lenses exceed 1.60, allowing for thinner, lighter designs with excellent optical clarity, making them ideal for high prescription strengths and fashionable eyewear.</p></p>
