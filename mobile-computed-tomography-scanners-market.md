<p><p>The mobile computed tomography (CT) scanners market is divided into three categories based on slice capacity. Low-slice scanners, with fewer than 64 slices, offer basic imaging capabilities and are often cost-effective for general diagnostics. Medium-slice scanners, featuring 64 slices, provide enhanced image quality and faster acquisition times, making them suitable for a wider range of clinical applications. High-slice scanners, with more than 64 slices, deliver advanced imaging with higher resolution and speed, ideal for complex procedures and specialized diagnostics.</p></p>
