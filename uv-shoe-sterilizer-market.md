<p><p>The UV shoe sterilizer market encompasses two primary types: in-shoe disinfection and sole disinfection. In-shoe disinfection focuses on sanitizing the entire interior of footwear, eliminating bacteria, viruses, and odors to enhance hygiene and comfort. Sole disinfection, on the other hand, targets the outer soles of shoes, addressing the accumulation of dirt and pathogens from various surfaces. Both types utilize UV technology to provide effective and chemical-free sterilization, catering to health-conscious consumers seeking cleanliness in their footwear.</p></p>
