<p><p>Float switch markets can be categorized into three main types: top-mounted, side-mounted, and special types. Top-mounted float switches are installed above the liquid level, detecting changes in liquid volume and are commonly used in tanks. Side-mounted float switches are fixed to the side of tanks and can monitor levels effectively, ideal for narrow spaces. Special type float switches include customized designs for unique applications, offering versatility in various industries like wastewater, HVAC, and industrial processes, ensuring efficient liquid management.</p></p>
