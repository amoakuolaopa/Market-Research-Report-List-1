<p><p>The Intrauterine Contraceptive Devices (IUCD) market is primarily divided into two types: Copper IUCDs and Hormonal IUCDs. Copper IUCDs utilize copper wire to create a toxic environment for sperm, offering long-lasting, hormone-free contraception. Hormonal IUCDs, on the other hand, release progestin, which thickens cervical mucus and thins the uterine lining, providing effective pregnancy prevention while potentially offering additional benefits like reduced menstrual bleeding. Both types cater to different user preferences and health needs, driving diverse market demand.</p></p>
