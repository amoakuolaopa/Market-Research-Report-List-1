<p><strong>The Flexible Alternating Current Transmission Systems (FACTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Compensation</li><li>Series Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible Alternating Current Transmission Systems (FACTS) enhance the efficiency and reliability of power transmission networks. The market types within FACTS include shunt compensation and series compensation. Shunt compensation, involving devices like Static Var Compensators (SVC), provides reactive power support and voltage stabilization at specific points in the grid. In contrast, series compensation, utilizing devices like Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitors (TCSC), improves power flow and reduces line congestion by dynamically adjusting impedance in transmission lines, optimizing overall system performance.</p></p>
