<p><p>The Pillow Filling Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: double-channel filling and single-channel filling. Double-channel filling machines allow for simultaneous filling of two pillow compartments, enhancing efficiency and productivity for high-volume operations. In contrast, single-channel filling machines handle one compartment at a time, making them suitable for smaller production needs or specialized items. Both types cater to varying production scales and requirements, ensuring flexibility for manufacturers in the textile and bedding industries.</p></p>
