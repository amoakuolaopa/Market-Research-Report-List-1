<p><ul><li>Latex-bonded Airlaid Napkin</li><li>Thermal-bonded Airlaid Napkin</li><li>Multi-bonded Airlaid Napkin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airlaid napkin market includes three primary types: latex-bonded, thermal-bonded, and multi-bonded airlaid napkins. Latex-bonded airlaid napkins are crafted using latex adhesives, offering flexibility and strength, making them suitable for various applications. Thermal-bonded airlaid napkins utilize heat to bond fibers, providing durability and excellent absorption properties. Multi-bonded airlaid napkins combine both latex and thermal bonding techniques, resulting in a versatile product that benefits from the strengths of both methods, enhancing performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
