<p><p>The Rotary Air Preheaters market primarily comprises two types: Drum-Type Rotary Air Preheaters and Windshield Rotary Air Preheaters. Drum-Type Rotary Air Preheaters utilize a rotating drum to heat incoming air with exhaust gases, enhancing combustion efficiency in power plants. Meanwhile, Windshield Rotary Air Preheaters feature a more compact design, often integrating with various systems to optimize heat exchange. Both types improve thermal efficiency, reduce fuel consumption, and contribute to lower emissions in industrial processes.</p></p>
