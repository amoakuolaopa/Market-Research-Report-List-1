<p><p>The PTFE films market is categorized into three main types: hydrophobic PTFE films, hydrophilic PTFE films, and others. Hydrophobic PTFE films repel water, making them ideal for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as in electrical insulation and protective coatings. Hydrophilic PTFE films, on the other hand, attract water, enhancing wicking and absorption, which is beneficial in medical and filtration products. The "others" category includes specialized PTFE films tailored for unique industrial applications, showcasing the material's versatility.</p></p>
