<p><p>The heating pad market comprises three main types: microwavable heating pads, electric heating pads, and chemical heating pads. Microwavable heating pads are filled with materials like rice or gel and are heated in a microwave for instant warmth. Electric heating pads provide adjustable heat through an electrical source and are ideal for longer usage. Chemical heating pads contain substances that produce heat through an exothermic reaction, offering portable and disposable warmth. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
