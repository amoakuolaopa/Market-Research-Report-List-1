Mass spectrometry provides detailed molecular information, crucial for identifying and quantifying substances in complex biological samples. Electrophoresis is utilized for separating nucleic acids and proteins based on size and charge, enabling genetic research and clinical diagnostics. Together, these techniques are vital for advancements in biotechnology and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
