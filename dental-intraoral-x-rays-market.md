<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental intraoral X-rays market comprises several types, including bitewing X-rays and periapical X-rays, among others. Bitewing X-rays are designed to capture the crowns of teeth in one area of the mouth, providing essential information for detecting cavities between teeth. Periapical X-rays focus on the entire tooth, including the root and surrounding bone, facilitating detailed assessment of periodontal health. Other types may include occlusal X-rays, which capture larger areas of the dental arch, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in clinical practice.</p></p>
