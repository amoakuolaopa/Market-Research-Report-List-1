<p><ul><li>AC Film Capacitor</li><li>DC Film Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The film capacitor market is divided into two main types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed to handle alternating current applications, often used in power electronics, filters, and motor drives due to their ability to withstand voltage fluctuations. In contrast, DC film capacitors are optimized for direct current applications, commonly found in power supplies, energy storage, and signal processing. Each type caters to specific electrical requirements and performance characteristics, influencing their applications across various industries.</p></p>
