High-frequency MLCCs are designed for applications requiring rapid signal transmission, commonly used in smartphones and communication devices. Low-frequency MLCCs cater to power applications, such as automotive and industrial equipment, where stability under lower frequencies is essential. Semiconductor MLCCs are specialized for integrated circuits, providing high capacitance in compact sizes, crucial for modern electronic devices. Each type addresses distinct performance requirements.</p></p>
