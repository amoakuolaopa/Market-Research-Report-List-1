<p><p>The Analog Design Services market encompasses two primary types: Forward Design and Reverse Design. Forward Design involves creating innovative analog circuits from scratch, focusing on meeting specific performance criteria and requirements. In contrast, Reverse Design entails analyzing existing circuits to understand their functionality, often for purposes of replication, improvement, or obsolescence management. Both approaches serve distinct roles in the development and enhancement of analog systems, catering to various industry needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
