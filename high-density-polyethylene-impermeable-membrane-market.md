<p><p>The High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) impermeable membrane market is categorized into thermoplastic and thermosetting plastics. Thermoplastic HDPE membranes are flexible, recyclable, and easily moldable, making them suitable for applications in landfill liners and waterproofing. In contrast, thermosetting plastics offer enhanced strength and durability, ideal for use in harsher environments, such as chemical storage and industrial applications. The distinction between these two types influences their production processes, performance characteristics, and end-use applications within the impermeable membrane market.</p></p>
