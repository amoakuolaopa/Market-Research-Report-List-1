<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed</li><li>Air-Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paralleling reactor market is segmented into two main types: oil-immersed and air-core reactors, each serving distinct applications in power systems. Oil-immersed reactors utilize oil for insulation and cooling, offering high efficiency and durability, making them suitable for large-scale installations. In contrast, air-core reactors rely on air for insulation, resulting in lighter and more compact designs. They are ideal for smaller applications and environments where space is limited. Both types play a critical role in enhancing power quality and stability.</p></p>
