<p><p>The fire alarm cable market includes three primary types: FPL (Fire Power Limited) cables, designed for general fire alarm installations, offering reliable performance in various environments. FPLP (Fire Power Limited Plenum) cables are specifically engineered for plenum spaces, where air circulation occurs, featuring a low-smoke and low-flame spread composition. FPLR (Fire Power Limited Riser) cables are optimized for vertical riser applications, ensuring safety in vertical installations while adhering to strict building codes for fire safety. Each type addresses specific installation needs and safety requirements.</p></p>
